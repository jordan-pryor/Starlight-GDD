## Basic Overview

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="Starlight" icon="game-controller">
		**Starlight** invites players to dive into a world where the echoes of a lost golden age collide with the harsh realities of a post-cataclysmic existence. With its deep story, immersive landscapes, and unique gameplay features, it offers a compelling experience for those seeking adventure, mystery, and a chance to restore humanity's lost glory. Explore vast open worlds, engage in thrilling battles, and uncover secrets that will change the course of history.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Concept" icon="lightbulb">
		**Genre:** Open-world RPG / FPS Pseudo-MMO<br>
		Starlight combines the expansive exploration and role-playing elements of open-world RPGs with the fast-paced, immersive action of first-person shooters. The pseudo-MMO aspect allows for dynamic, large-scale interactions with other players without the need for persistent online connectivity.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Target Audience" icon="target">
		**Age:** 15-30<br>
		**Gender:** Male/Female<br>
		Starlight is designed for a diverse audience, appealing to both younger and older gamers who enjoy deep narratives, complex characters, and strategic gameplay. The blend of RPG and FPS elements aims to attract fans from both genres.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Monetization" icon="dollar-sign">
		**Freemium Model:**<br>
		Starlight will be free-to-play with optional in-game micro-transactions. Players can purchase cosmetic items, experience boosts, and other non-essential enhancements. The monetization strategy ensures a fair and enjoyable experience for both free and paying players, with all essential game content accessible without spending money.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Platforms & System Requirements" icon="device">
		**Platforms:**<br>
		- PC: Windows & Linux<br>
		- **To be determined:** Potential expansion to consoles and other platforms based on demand and development feasibility.
		<br><br>
		**System Requirements (Tentative):**<br>
		- **Minimum:**<br>
			- OS: Windows 10 64-bit / Ubuntu 20.04<br>
			- Processor: Intel i5 / AMD equivalent<br>
			- Memory: 8 GB RAM<br>
			- Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 970 / AMD equivalent<br>
			- DirectX: Version 11<br>
			- Storage: 50 GB available space
		<br><br>
		- **Recommended:**<br>
			- OS: Windows 10 64-bit / Ubuntu 20.04<br>
			- Processor: Intel i7 / AMD equivalent<br>
			- Memory: 16 GB RAM<br>
			- Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1070 / AMD equivalent<br>
			- DirectX: Version 12<br>
			- Storage: 50 GB available space
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
